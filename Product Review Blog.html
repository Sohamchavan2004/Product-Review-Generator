Verdict

Overall Rating: 4.5/5 stars

Who should buy: Users who prioritize a top-tier display, fast performance, and a feature-rich experience, and are willing to pay a premium.

Who should avoid: Those on a tight budget, heavy gamers concerned about heating, or users who prioritize long battery life above all else.

The Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra is a powerhouse of a phone, offering a premium experience with a few compromises. While the price and battery life may deter some, its exceptional display, performance, and camera capabilities make it a top contender in the flagship smartphone market.
